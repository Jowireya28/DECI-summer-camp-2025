2-Minute Stretching Exercise

Let’s get your body moving and your mind refreshed!

Stretching helps your body feel relaxed and your brain feel ready to learn. Take a break and follow these simple steps:

  • Neck Rolls – Slowly roll your head in a circle. 5 seconds each way.
  • Shoulder Shrugs – Lift your shoulders up to your ears and drop them. Do this 5 times.
  • Arm Reaches – Reach both arms up to the sky and take a deep breath in. Lower them slowly while breathing out. Repeat 3 times.
  • Side Stretch – Put your right hand on your hip and stretch your left arm over your head to the right side. Hold for 5 seconds. Switch sides.
  • Shake It Out! – Shake your hands, feet, arms, and legs for 10 seconds to release any tension.

Great job!

You just did something great for your body and mind. Now you’re ready for your next activity!
