Let's do some Breathing Exercise



Take a deep breath... and relax!

Here, you’ll learn fun and easy ways to calm your mind using breathing. These exercises help you when you feel stressed, angry, or nervous.
Try these simple steps:

Exercise 1: Balloon Breathing

  • Sit comfortably and close your eyes.
  • Take a deep breath in through your nose as if you are blowing up a balloon.
  • Hold it for 3 seconds.
  • Slowly blow the air out through your mouth like you're letting the air out of a balloon.
  • Repeat 5 times.

Tip: Imagine the balloon getting bigger every time you breathe in

Exercise 2: Wave Breathing

  • Sit or lie down.
  • Breathe in slowly for 4 seconds.
  • Hold your breath for 4 seconds.
  • Breathe out slowly for 4 seconds.
  • Rest for 4 seconds.
  • Repeat like the rhythm of ocean waves.

Great for relaxing before bed!

Exercise 3: Smell the Flower, Blow the Candle

  • Imagine you’re holding a flower in one hand and a candle in the other.
  • Breathe in slowly like you’re smelling the flower
  • Breathe out gently like you’re blowing out the candle
  • Repeat 5 times.
