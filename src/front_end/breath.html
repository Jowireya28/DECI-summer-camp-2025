Exercise 1: Balloon Breathing

  • Sit comfortably and close your eyes.
  • Take a deep breath in through your nose as if you are blowing up a balloon.
  • Hold it for 3 seconds.
  • Slowly blow the air out through your mouth like you're letting the air out of a balloon.
  • Repeat 5 times.

Tip: Imagine the balloon getting bigger every time you breathe in
